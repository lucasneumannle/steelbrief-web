What you get

Price & Production Brief
Steel price momentum (hot-rolled and cold-rolled indices, month-over-month and year-over-year) alongside weekly domestic output and capacity utilization. A factual read of where the market stands.
  • BLS producer price indices via FRED
  • AISI weekly production & utilization
  • Descriptive market read — no hype
Energy Cost & Tariff Watch
Henry Hub natural gas and industrial electricity — the leading cost inputs for electric-arc-furnace mills — plus live USITC steel-product proceedings: antidumping reviews, safeguards, and Section 232 activity.
  • EIA energy benchmarks
  • USITC trade actions via Federal Register
  • Early signal on cost & supply risk

Every week's data stays in your sheet and builds a clean, consolidated time series no free source keeps in one place. Track the full cycle and time your contracts against real history — not just last week's number.
